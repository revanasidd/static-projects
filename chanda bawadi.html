CHANDA BAWADI

  1. It was built by adil shah in the year 1557-1580 on the eastern borders of bijpur city.

  2. Adil shah named this tank after his wife Chand Bibi.

  3. Chand bawdai was constructed with storage capcity of 20 Million litres to provide water to the people.

  4. sometime later this tank became an ideal model for other tanks built in the city of bijapur.

  5. A big compound was constructed around the tank for maintenance staff. however the complex was occasionally used by members of the Royal family for recreation purposes
